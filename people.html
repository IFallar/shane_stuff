The Tetrarchy

At the end of the Crisis of the Third Century, Roman Emperor Diocletian, hoping to avoid a repeat of the crises instituted a new form of government: The Tetrachy. This is the story of four men who would rebuild Rome, and eventually... tear it apart

Crusader Kings

The armies of Christendom and Islam once again march to war. Come to know the likes of Saladin, Richard Lionheart, Phillip II ,and Frederick Barbarossa in this so called King's Crusade...

Brothers Gracchi

As Rome grew, the wars enriched the wealthy and impoverished the soldiers who fought in them. Into these times were born the brothers Tiberius and Gaius Gracchus, who in their efforts to save the Roman Republic, would ultimately seal its fate...

The Great Northern War

The Boy King of Sweden and the Emperor of Russia, two men who would be known as "the Great", and whose rivalry would engulf the frozen north in the fires of war, a war that will break an empire and herald the rise of another...

Men of Iron

These are the first crusaders, the first group of men who would take it upon themselves to carry the cross and retake the holy land. The men who would ignite nearly 200 years of war, and forever define the high middle ages... Deus Vult!

Iron Chancellor

The life of the man who would go on to become the greatest statesman of a generation, the man who would use Europe for a chessboard and redraw its map forever, Otto von Bismarck, the man who would go on to unite Germany.
